About News Analyzer

This web app makes your life easy. Just provide the URL of a news article, and it will give you a complete analysis.

You'll see the title, the cleaned article, and even an estimate to read the article, the number of sentences and more.

Whether you're a news enthusiast, a researcher, or someone who just wants a quick summary, News Analyzer is here to enhance your news-reading experience. Enjoy hassle-free insights and stay informed with ease!
